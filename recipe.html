Mac n Cheese Pasta

A quick, creamy mac and cheese perfect for busy days. Made with simple ingredients and ready in 25 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 box elbow macaroni
  • Â¼ cup butter + flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups cheddar cheese
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Boil macaroni until firm, then drain.
  2. Make a butter-flour roux; add milk.
  3. Add cheese, season, mix till smooth.
  4. Combine with macaroni and serve.
